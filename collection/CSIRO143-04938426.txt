ListenCold water eddy ‘monsters” mighty current off Sydney (Podcast 20 Mar 07)In this six-minute podcast Dr David Griffin, from the Wealth from Ocean's Flagship, explains how a huge dense eddy was the reason the sea was so cold off the coast of NSW this summer. DataTraceDNA takes up fight against fake art fraudsters (Podcast 2 Mar 07)Mr Greg Twemlow from DataTraceDNA explains how technology developed by CSIRO is tackling the problem of fake art in this six-minute podcast. Keeping track of Australia’s water (Podcast 20 Feb 07)Dr Rob Vertessy discusses the Water Resources Observation Network (WRON) in this five-minute podcast, and why it’s so important to Australia. Gigabit wireless breakthrough (Podcast 13 Dec 06)Dr Jay Guo discusses a new wireless six gigabit connection which uses millimetre waves technology in this three-minute podcast. Award-winning Air Cargo Scanner (video)This two-minute video discusses the work of Dr Brian Sowerby, Chief Research Scientist at CSIRO Minerals, and the team that developed the Air Cargo Scanner for the Detection of Contraband in Air Cargo which won the team a 2006 CSIRO Medal for Research Achievement. Playing the air guitar (Podcast 13 Nov 06)Dr Richard Helmer talks about a shirt that lets the user play real music on an air guitar in this five-minute podcast. Great white shark’s incredible journey (Podcast 2 Nov 06)Mr Barry Bruce talks about a female great white shark who travelled thousands of kilometres around the Australian coast in this seven-minute podcast. Continuous media web project (video)A one-minute video looks at the Continuous Media Web (CMWeb) - a project that is developing a platform to generically perform annotation and hyperlinking of fragments of time-continuously sampled data in a Web-integrated manner.
