Publication - General   A history of food technology in Australia bookTo Feed a Nation, a book from CSIRO PUBLISHING, highlights how a growing understanding of the nature of food, the principles of nutrition, and the role of micro-organisms, was able to propel food technology to where it is today. Alumina to Zirconia bookAlumina to Zirconia is a history of the CSIRO Division of Mineral Chemistry, a predecessor of today's CSIRO Minerals. Australian Orchid Genera: an information and identification systemThis CD-ROM is a complete information and identification system for Australian orchids containing the most comprehensive collection to date of data on all Australian orchid genera, with outstanding photographs of all aspects of each species. Australian Soils and Landscapes: an illustrated compendiumAn illustrated compendium that provides an introduction to the remarkable soils and landscapes of Australia. Australian seafood handbookThe authors have made a huge contribution to the knowledge and understanding of domestic seafood species, creating an essential reference for anyone who deals with Australian seafood. Australian seafood handbook: An identification guide to imported species bookA comprehensive, full-colour guide to all major imported seafood species in Australia. CSIRO list of Australian vertebrates bookAn indispensable reference for all people working with and publishing on Australia’s vertebrate fauna. Climate Change: Turning up the Heat bookIs climate change really happening and does it matter? The answer from the scientific community is a resounding yes. Barrie Pittock, one of the world’s leading climate researchers, argues that we need to act urgently to avoid increasingly severe climate change. Complete field guide to dragonflies of Australia bookDragonflies and damselflies, with their kaleidescope of colours and incredible flying abilities, are one of the most watchable kinds of animals. With stunning full-colour images, this book covers all 30 families, 110 genera and 324 species found in Australia. EUCLID: eucalypts of Australia DVDThe definitive electronic identification and information system for Australian eucalypts. Ecohydrology: Vegetation function, water and resource managementThis book describes and provides a synthesis of the different disciplines required to understand the sustainable management of water in the environment. Engineering a Legacy bookEngineering a Legacy recounts the memories of the people who founded, guided and led CSIRO’s Chemical Engineering stream before it converged with Mineral Products in 1995 to form CSIRO Minerals. Fields of Discovery: Australia's CSIRO bookCelebrates the inspiring and often dramatic journey of one of the world’s most enduring scientific bodies. Finishing wool fabrics guideTechniques described in this book have been gathered over 15 years and will give readers a better understanding of the physical and chemical mechanisms of wool finishing. Forest trees of AustraliaForest trees of Australia is the essential reference to 300 of our most important indigenous trees, which have been carefully selected for their environmental significance, their importance to the timber industry, or their prominence in our landscape. From fleece to fabric bookFrom Fleece to Fabric provides an overview of the wool industry from 1948–1998 and how research by CSIRO’s Wool Technology group helped create one of the nation’s main export industries. Growing crops with reclaimed wastewater bookThis book provides an overview of wastewater treatment processes in Australia and evaluates the suitability of different irrigation systems for horticultural crops using reclaimed water. In search of sustainability bookAchieving sustainability will require major changes in our current approaches. The thought-provoking chapters in this book provide a solid introduction to the issues in the search for a genuine path to sustainability. Landscape and building design for bushfire areas bookThis book encourages design for bushfire to be included as a normal part of designing in bushfire-prone areas. It will assist planning and building regulatory authorities to improve and administer regulatory requirements and guidelines. Managing and conserving grassy woodlands bookEdited by experts from CSIRO Sustainable Ecosystems, this book provides the principles for landholders to maintain or increase productivity without compromising ecological sustainability. New Forests: Wood Production and Environmental Services bookCovers issues relevant to the development of new forests, including soils, the bio-physical environment, water use and irrigation strategies. The CSIRO Total Wellbeing Diet BookCSIRO's Total Wellbeing Diet has now been produced as a book - published by Penguin - chock full of delectable recipes for a weight loss program that is successful and provides the nutrients required for health and vitality. The Lennox legacy: the history of the CSIRO laboratory at 343 Royal Parade Parkville bookThe Lennox legacy: the history of the CSIRO laboratory at 343 Royal Parade Parkville records many of the events and incidents associated with the genesis and development of the Division of Molecular and Health Technologies over more than fifty years.
