Feature Article   Dr Rob Vertessy: tackling Australia's land and water challengesChief of CSIRO Land and Water, Rob Vertessy, discusses his background, his research interests and his current work. Flagships: science tackling the big challengesDr Ron Sandland, Deputy Chief Executive and Chair of the Flagship Oversight Committee, has written an article titled: Flagships: science tackling the big challenges.  He describes the reasons for creating Flagships, how they were designed, how they are measured, and how they will evolve. Avon River Basin 2050: Confronting our futureThis is a 20-page report presents the outcomes of this project in 'plain English' for a general audience detailing the explored alternative future landscapes for the wheatbelt of Western Australia. CSIRO Land and Water: corporate profile reportThis 16-page corporate profile outlines the research objectives, expertise and capacity of CSIRO’s Land and Water division.
